This repository contains a report and spreadsheet describing the technical and logistical design for a 20 person cooperative
that produces automotive fuel from stale bakery waste.  To date, this material is a paper design only.  The design is based upon 
experimental work performed in 2012 by Bob Glicksman, Lucy Geever and Mark Kent, see:
(https://github.com/BobGlicksman/FeedstockProcessingExperiments/).
